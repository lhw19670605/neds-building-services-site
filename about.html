About Us

NORTHERN EDUCATIONAL DEVELOPMENT SERVICES Inc. is a Not-For-Profit business located in Middletown, New York. Established in August 2024, its focus is to support educational and religious institutions in the local area through providing at-cost construction services and any other services that may be beneficial and useful to such institutions.
